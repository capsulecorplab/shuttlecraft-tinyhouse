= Shuttlecraft Tinyhouse

The Shuttlecraft Tinyhouse is an open-source https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tiny_house_movement[tiny house] designed to emulate the experience of living in a https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shuttlecraft[shuttlecraft]. Inspired by futuristic spacefaring vehicles (s.a., the Star Trek https://memory-alpha.fandom.com/wiki/Runabout[Runabout]), the Shuttlecraft Tinyhouse is commissioned to explore alternative, affordable, and sustainable living solutions for modern households who dream of living among the stars :sparkles:
